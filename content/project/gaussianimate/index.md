GaussiAnimate is a scaffold-skin rigging system for reconstructing and animating diverse categories from 4D Gaussian or mesh sequences.

<!--more-->

It introduces the skelebone representation and Partwise Motion Matching for controllable yet expressive reanimation. The project page includes the paper, teaser, method overview, and extensive qualitative results.
